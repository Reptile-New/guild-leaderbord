BWoCC Guild Boards

PvP circuit

Guild PvP ranking driven by tournament placements — plus the best in-game ladder ratings (1v1 & 2v2 ranked) claimed by each guild.

Tournament circuit

Points earned across inter-guild tournaments (major: 100/60/45/35/20 — standard: 50/30/22/18/10). Computed automatically from tournament results.

In-game ladders

Best rating per guild on the ranked ladders (declared by guild masters, screenshot/video for verification). Informative — does not feed the circuit points.
